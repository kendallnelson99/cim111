How to make Mini Oreo Cheesecake

Ingredients

  • 16 oz of Cream cheese
  • 1/4 cup of sour cream
  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 12 oreos & extra for toppings

Step 1

Combine cream cheese, sour cream, sugar, vanilla, and eggs in a large mixing bowl

Step 2

Place 1 oreo in cupcake liner

Step 3

Pour in cheesecake batter

Step 4

Crush desired amount of oreos in a bag and place on top of Cheesecake as topping

Step 5

Bake in Oven at 275 degrees for 22 minutes

Step 6

Once out of the oven, let cool completely before putting in the fridge to chill for 3 hours before consumption
